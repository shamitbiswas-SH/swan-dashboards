Methodology — what the agent does on every run
  • Detect: scan for job postings from the last 7 days matching benefits configuration, provider data, product/plan ops, claims configuration at US payers. Max 5 companies per run, daily at 8:20 AM.
  • Verify & research: confirm genuine payer; cheapest data first (own records → free sources → paid enrichment only if a gap remains); every claim source-cited with confidence flags.
  • Score: signal strength × deal size → Gold / Silver / Bronze; job postings gain weight when corroborated (named platform, multiple roles, migration); weak accounts stop before any paid enrichment; scores decay without fresh signals.
  • Find the owner: VP/Director of Configuration, Provider Data, Product Ops or IT/Ops; verified email where possible, LinkedIn otherwise.
  • Draft: hook-anchored sequences (3–5 steps, new angle per follow-up, soft close); "no hook, no send"; auto-send disabled.
  • Stage for review: approve / edit / adjust / wait — nothing sends without a human.
