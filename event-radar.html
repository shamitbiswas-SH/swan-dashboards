How this dataset was built (agent methodology)
  • Scope: 10 competitors × 3 years × 12 flagship payer events always checked by name, plus each competitor's own circuit.
  • Sources: event exhibitor/sponsor directories and competitor site crawls (high confidence, Wayback fallback); press wires, trade media, LinkedIn and YouTube scrapes (medium); HubSpot competitive fields once connected.
  • Rules: only URLs actually retrieved that explicitly name competitor + event + year; no extrapolating future years; exhaustive sweep per competitor; summary counts must reconcile exactly with detail rows.
  • Runs: Wave 1 (Jul 1) — HealthEdge, Innovaccer, Cotiviti, Symplr; an early pass surfaced 4 URL-integrity problems, the skill was revised and re-run before staging. Wave 2 (Jul 6) — remaining six, on directory/site/press sources only (social scrapers unavailable).
  • Governance: research only — nothing is sent externally; output staged for human review.
